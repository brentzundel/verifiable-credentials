Group Communications

Mailing lists

  • public-vc-wg@w3.org: the group’s primary mailing list. Group members are automatically signed up. The archives are publicly visible, and anyone can post messages.
  • member-vc-wg@w3.org: the group’s member confidential administrative mailing list. Group participants are automatically signed up. The archives are visible to W3C members and group participants only, and only group participants can post a mail.
  • group-vc-wg-chairs@w3.org: list for chairs and W3C staff contact(s). The archives are only visible to the chairs and the W3C staff, but anybody can post a mail.
